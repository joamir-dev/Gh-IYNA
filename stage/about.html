About Us

The Gharbiya Chapter of the International Youth Neuroscience Association strives to promote accessible neuroscience education locally through hands-on seminars, workshops, and mentored research initiatives.

All programs are held virtually or in centrally located community centers to ensure widespread participation regardless of background or location. The chapter aims to spark curiosity in the brain sciences, inspire future innovators and foster connections between motivated youth and established researchers.

Led by a passionate team committed to education equity, the chapter cultivates the next generation of neuroscience leaders from all communities. Find out more about the passionate staff and volunteers running the organization below!
